About Me

Posted by Miguel Grinberg under Personal.
Tweet

Hi, my name is Miguel, and I live in Portland, Oregon, USA. I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I'm a software engineer but also tinker with photography and filmmaking when I have time.

I'm happily married to a woman that has a pretty succesful blog of her own, and I'm a father of four kids, two girls and two boys.

The software that powers this blog was all written by myself. It seemed like a pretty interesting project to write a blog engine from scratch, and now that I have a blog software that I like, I might as well use it!

The topics that I will be blogging about are going to be mostly related to one of two things: programming or photography. Or maybe not, at this point I don't really know.

Thanks for stopping by, I hope you find something useful or interesting. If you do, be sure to leave a comment.

Miguel
